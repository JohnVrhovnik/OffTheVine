Tastings

Walk-In Tastings – No Reservations Required

We offer three different settings for our walk-in tastings. Sample our famous Geneva Ohio wines and selected hand-crafted, winery-only Spotlight wines.

Visit the To Kalon Tasting Room for Reserve wines and personal service in a luxurious, intimate setting. Our Spotlight Wine Club members enjoy exclusive access to limited-release wines in their private tasting room, the Spotlight Wine Club Lounge.

Vineyard Room
Sample selected Classic and Spotlight wines, or enjoy wines by the glass. Prices vary.
Kalon Room
Sample four of our award-winning Reserve wines. $45 per person.
Spotlight Wine Club Lounge
Complimentary tasting for wine club members and up to three guests. $20 for each additional guest.

Hours

Year round. Tasting Rooms open daily from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., with last pour at 4:45 p.m.

*Reservations are required for parties of 8 or more. Please call 330-672-3031 for more information.

Signature Tours and Tastings

A 90-minute experience featuring a walk through our vineyards and cellars, concluding with a sit-down guided wine tasting.

Duration: 90 Minutes Price: $35 Per Person

This in-depth winery tour follows the path of the grape from the vineyard to the cellar to the finished wine. Guests walk into the Off the Vine, visit the winemaking cellars (including fermentation and barrel aging) and receive a seated educational tasting of three wines with one of our wine education specialists. Advanced reservations are recommended.

Hours

Year round, with increased frequency due to seasonality

Notes

Limited to 15 guests

Advanced reservations are recommended. Due to the nature of this tour, children under 13 years of age are not permitted.
